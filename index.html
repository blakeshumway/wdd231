About Me

My name is Blake Shumway, I was born in 2002 which also happens to be the same year that my favorite super hero trilogy began, which is Sam Raimi's Spider-man! Some of my favorite pastimes are hanging out with my friends, playing video games, reading books, studying the scriptures and watching movies! I served as a missionary for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in California Anaheim for 2 years. It was during that time that I developed a much stronger testimony of Jesus Christ and His gospel.
