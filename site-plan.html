Pokehackia Site Plan

Site Name: Pokehackia

This website is about pokemon romhacks and so uses both 'poke' and 'hack' to help convey what the website will contain. the 'ia' part is taken from the word 'topia' which denotes a place, so Pokehackia can be read as a place to find pokemon hacks.

Site Purpose

This site serves as an ideal starting point to learn more about romhacking for generation 4 pokemon games. This website will provide the viewer with information about what gen 4 rom hacking is, what tools are available for gen 4 rom hacking, a list of popular and recommended rock hacks and a form thats allows the viewer to sign up for a monthly newsletter that gives news on upcoming gen 4 romhacks.

Scenarios

"What are some of the best generation 4 romhacks?"

"What tools are needed/available for gen 4 romhacking?"
